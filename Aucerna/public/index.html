Model Complex Production
Systems in Minutes, Not Hours
Aucerna Integrated Production Modeling (IPM) software answers whether it can be done, and how. Its powerful modeling capabilities automatically incorporate reservoir pressure, depletion, and well designs into predictions, and resulting deliverability, while considering the capacities and flow impacts of surface facilities, pipelines, and market demand.
Aucerna’s IPM software is fast. It quickly and accurately history matches, forecasts, and production profiles, and it manages subsurface uncertainty quantification by generating the vast number of scenarios required for short, medium, and long-term production planning. Complex production systems with scores of wells and reservoirs can be solved in minutes rather than hours, allowing your team to optimize workflows and focus on value maximization.
Aucerna delivers comprehensive oil and compositional gas production modeling and forecasting for the modern energy industry.
Discover our Benefits
Rapid solutions to complex problems
Aucerna Integrated Production Modeling software solves complex problems in minutes, rather than the hours or days your teams would require with other tools. Rapid, parallelized calculations leave your team with more productive time to perform full optimization and analysis across a variety of what-if scenarios, while also developing a greater understanding of the asset. The speed of our software makes it appropriate for both long-term planning and short-term operational decision making.
One tool to model your complete production system
Aucerna Integrated Production Modeling software employs a market-facing optimization approach that enables you to meet contractual obligations while prioritizing the wells within your individually defined operational limits. The simulations model your entire production system from multiple reservoirs, wells, subsea or surface pipelines, compressors, and separators, to the market, while accounting for thermal and hydraulic considerations. This ensures that your data is only entered one time, in one system, and it eliminates the need for QA/QC validation of inputs across multiple modules, or systems.
Solve the whole problem
Aucerna Integrated Production Modeling Software also considers your compositional gas elements, avoiding the need to post-process these elements into your results, and all while maintaining its blazing fast speed. Accounting for these items intrinsically within the simulation leads to faster decisions and time to value – and eliminates error-prone post-processing work to approximate these complexities into hundreds of simulation outcomes.
Modern and Intuitive Interface
Aucerna Integrated Production Modeling software features a modern and easy-to-use interface, making maintaining evergreen models easier than ever. With clear inputs, interactions, and analysis tooling, it is easy to learn for rapid onboarding, implementation, and maintenance of your models.
